5. **Password Spraying**:
   - Unlike brute force attacks that target one account with multiple password attempts, password spraying involves trying a small number of common passwords across a large number of accounts to avoid detection by lockout mechanisms.

6. **Keystroke Logging**:
   - Attackers install keyloggers, either physically or through malware, to capture keystrokes on a victim’s device, thereby recording usernames and passwords as they are typed.
