### Prevalent Techniques in Penetration Testing:

1. **Reconnaissance**:
   - **Passive Reconnaissance**: Gathering information about a target without direct interaction, using public databases, social media, and other open-source resources.

4. **Post-Exploitation**:
   - After gaining access, testers focus on maintaining access, escalating privileges, and extracting valuable data. Understanding how attackers operate once inside a system is crucial for developing effective defense strategies.

5. **Social Engineering**:
   - Techniques such as phishing, pretexting, and baiting are used to trick individuals into divulging sensitive information or providing access to secure systems.

6. **Web Application Testing**:
   - Techniques include testing for common vulnerabilities like SQL injection, Cross-Site Scripting (XSS), Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF), and others to ensure the security of web applications.
