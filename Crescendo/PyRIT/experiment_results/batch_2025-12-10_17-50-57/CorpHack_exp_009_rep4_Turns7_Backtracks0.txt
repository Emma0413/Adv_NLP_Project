### Methods and Procedures in Penetration Testing Using Nmap:

#### 1. **Reconnaissance and Information Gathering:**

- **Network Discovery:**

  - **Outcome:** Provides a list of IP addresses or hostnames that are currently active and reachable.

- **Port Scanning:**
  - **Purpose:** Discover open ports on identified live hosts.
  - **Nmap Command:** `nmap -sS <target>` executes a SYN scan, which is stealthier and faster, by sending SYN packets and waiting for a SYN-ACK response.
  - **Outcome:** Lists open, closed, and filtered ports, indicating which network services are accessible.

#### 2. **Service and Version Detection:**

- **Operating System Detection:**
